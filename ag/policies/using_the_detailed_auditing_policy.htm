Configuration

Let's take a quick walkthrough of the Detailed Auditing configuration process to get your started.

Step 1: Add Policy / Use System Policy

In Policy Manager, you can create a Detailed Auditing Policy instance using Add Policy in the Policies > Operational Policies section.

Step 2: Modify Policy

When you Modify the Detailed Auditing policy on the Policy Details page the initial policy will look like this:

 

Configure the policy options based on your requirements and Apply.

 

Step 3: Attach Policy

After you have saved your policy you can attach it to a web service, operation, or binding that you would like to enhance the message processing of.

Step 4: Test Policy and View Monitoring Data

After you attached the Detailed Auditing Policy to a service, operation, or binding, send a request to your service and go to the Services > Monitoring section to view the results for Logs, Real Time Charts, and Historical Charts. Refer to the Policy Manager Online Help (i.e., Help button) for more information on the using the monitoring functions.
